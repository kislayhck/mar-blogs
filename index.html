SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING- WHAT IS IT?

Social Media are a set of online platforms where people throughout the world connect, share ideas and thought, create and follow content. On a lighter note it may seem that these social media networking provides just entertainment and serves leisure, but on taking a closer look we find a huge number of business that flourishes through these social media sites. The major social media platforms, such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, etc. have a huge number of registered business in them. These online business platforms, when apply the correct strategy can reach heights in their business by attracting their target audience and providing the relevant services. So, the question is, should your business have a social media page too? In order to answer this question, we need to understand the concept of SMM or Social Media Marketing.

What is SMM?

Social Media Marketing refers to the process of content creation to attract more audience to your business on the social networking sites like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.. SMM provides a platform to showcase your products and services and lets you have an interactive session with your clients. The insights and analysis provided by Social Medias, gives you a clear picture of what is working and what isn't, how many people visit your website and how many keep themselves limited to the posts.
